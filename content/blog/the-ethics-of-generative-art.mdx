Generative art lives in a gray area between training data and creative
inspiration. The same mechanism that lets a model produce something novel also
ties it inextricably to the work it learned from.

## Where does inspiration end and replication begin?

Human artists absorb influences too — but a model can memorize and regurgitate at
a scale and fidelity that humans cannot. That difference matters when we talk
about consent and attribution.

## Building responsibly

- Prefer datasets with clear licensing.
- Give creators ways to opt out.
- Be transparent about how outputs are generated.

The technology is not going away. The question is whether we build it in a way we
can be proud of.
